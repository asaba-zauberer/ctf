Internal Fetcher (Whitelisted)

This diagnostic fetcher only supports ECS task metadata credentials endpoints.

Usage:

GET /c/ecs-ssrf-fetcher/api/fetch?sessionId=<SID>&url=http://169.254.170.2/v2/credentials/<uuid>

Other internal/external URLs are blocked by design.

In production, this endpoint should be disabled or require mTLS and strict audit logging.
